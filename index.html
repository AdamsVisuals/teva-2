Our Story

Founded in 2015 and headquartered in Arusha, Tanzania, Teva Eco Travel is East Africa's leading sustainable safari and eco-tourism company. Our mission is to provide transformative travel experiences while preserving Tanzania's natural beauty and supporting local communities. Our vision is to become the global standard for sustainable African tourism that benefits wildlife, ecosystems, and local communities.

Our team of expert guides and hospitality professionals share a passion for preserving natural wonders while creating unforgettable experiences for our guests. Each journey is designed to foster deep connections with people, wildlife and landscapes.

Our Commitment

Sustainability & Conservation Initiatives

At Teva Eco Travel, we don't just talk about sustainability - we live it. These are the concrete actions we're taking to protect the ecosystems we love.

Serengeti Rhino Protection Program

Partnership with Tanzania Wildlife Authority, anti-poaching patrol support, habitat restoration, and community education programs have achieved a 27% increase in black rhino population since 2018.

Maasai Community Development

Educational scholarships, sustainable income opportunities, women's entrepreneurship programs, healthcare initiatives, and traditional knowledge preservation projects are empowering the Maasai community.

Zanzibar Marine Conservation

Coral reef restoration, sustainable fishing education, plastic pollution reduction, marine protected area development, and sea turtle conservation are safeguarding Zanzibar's marine ecosystems.

Kilimanjaro Porter Welfare

Fair wages, proper equipment, educational opportunities, healthcare benefits, and career advancement pathways ensure the well-being of Kilimanjaro porters and guides.

Carbon Offsetting Program

Comprehensive carbon calculation, local reforestation projects, renewable energy initiatives, emissions reduction strategies, and transparent reporting are reducing our carbon footprint.
